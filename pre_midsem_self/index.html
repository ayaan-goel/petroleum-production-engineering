Problem 01

Productivity Test & AOF Calculation

Problem Statement

Analysis & Methodology

Given:

  • \( q = 110 \text{ STB/day} \)
  • \( P_{wf} = 900 \text{ psi} \)
  • \( \bar{P}_R = 1,300 \text{ psi} \)

Calculations:

  1. Productivity Index (PI or J):
    \( J = \frac{q}{\bar{P}_R - P_{wf}} = \frac{110}{1300 - 900} = \frac{110}{400} = \mathbf{0.275 \text{ STB/day/psi}} \)
  2. Absolute Open Flow (AOF):
    \( AOF = J \times \bar{P}_R = 0.275 \times 1300 = \mathbf{357.5 \text{ STB/day}} \)
  3. Oil flow rate at \( P_{wf} = 600 \text{ psi} \):
    \( q = J \times (\bar{P}_R - P_{wf}) = 0.275 \times (1300 - 600) = 0.275 \times 700 = \mathbf{192.5 \text{ STB/day}} \)
  4. Wellbore flowing pressure required to produce 250 STB/day:
    \( P_{wf} = \bar{P}_R - \frac{q}{J} = 1300 - \frac{250}{0.275} = 1300 - 909.09 = \mathbf{390.9 \text{ psi}} \)
Problem 02

Saturated Oil Reservoir & IPR Curve

Problem Statement

Analysis & Methodology

Given:

  • \( \bar{P}_R = 3,000 \text{ psig} \)
  • \( q = 400 \text{ STB/day} \)
  • \( P_{wf} = 2,580 \text{ psig} \)

Calculations:

  1. Productivity Index (J):
    \( J = \frac{q}{\bar{P}_R - P_{wf}} = \frac{400}{3000 - 2580} = \frac{400}{420} = \mathbf{0.952 \text{ STB/day/psi}} \)
  2. Oil flow rate at \( P_{wf} = 1950 \text{ psig} \):
    \( q = J \times (\bar{P}_R - P_{wf}) = 0.95238 \times (3000 - 1950) = 0.95238 \times 1050 = \mathbf{1000 \text{ STB/day}} \)
  3. Absolute Open Flow (AOF):
    \( AOF = J \times \bar{P}_R = 0.95238 \times 3000 = \mathbf{2857.1 \text{ STB/day}} \)
